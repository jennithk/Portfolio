IoT-Based Smart Waste Management

Click to see detailed description of this project, including AI chatbot integration and user-engagement leaderboard.

Agenda: To create a prototype of an intelligent waste management system that combines a smart dustbin with an AI chatbot and a user-engagement leaderboard, promoting better sorting habits and environmental awareness.

Problem: Current waste management systems are inefficient, leading to improper waste segregation at the source, creating contaminated waste streams and environmental/health hazards. Lack of user engagement and awareness.

Solution: AI-integrated Smart Waste Management System automates segregation of dry, wet, and e-waste, incentivizing proper disposal through a gamified web platform.

Construction: Sensors detect hand movements and classify waste type/moisture. Users have individual web logins. AI chatbot reviews waste and provides suggestions. Leaderboard encourages proper usage. Ultrasonic, IR, and moisture sensors handle detection and segregation.

Key Aspects: AI integration, global leaderboard, accurate segregation.

Expected Outcomes: Smarter waste segregation, reduced pollution, monitoring daily waste, promoting correct disposal.

Differences from Existing Models: AI system for user-specific review, global leaderboard, smart connectivity.
